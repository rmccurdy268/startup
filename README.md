Elevator Pitch:
  I got into Magic the Gathering this last summer. My Brother and I have become addicted to it; the world, the art, the strategy and the comptetition. Sometimes the rules can be confusing. I'm building an application that helps lay out those rules for beginners. If you ever need help understanding a rule or a keyword on a card, the app will help you understand how the card or mechanic works. There's also a social aspect so you can keep up with your friends and which cards they are putting in their decks. Learning Magic just got so much easier!
  

![IMG_2056](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/90003677/214464941-10372a80-3aac-4414-8091-ad2827030739.jpg)

KEY FEATURES:
  -Login to keep an account with your decks, friends and favorite cards.
  -Card lookup and Keyword lookup to help MTG players understand the cards and rules of the game
  -Links to helpful websites to teach users about the cards or purchase them from trusted card retailers.
